Masala Dosai

Description

Masala Dosai is an easy and popular, tasty south indian staple breakfast recipe made with rice and urad dal. basically an extension to the traditional dosa recipe, where dosa is made crisp and stuffed with potato masala. it is perhaps one of the famous dishes from south india, which is can be served for morning breakfast or also as an evening snack with coconut chutney and sambar.

Ingredients

For battar
  • 3 cup sona masuri rice
  • 1/2 tsp methi / fenugreek seeds
  • water (for soaking)
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 2 tbsp toor dal
  • 2 tbsp chana dal
  • 1 cup poha / avalakki
For Aloo Bhajji
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard
  • 1 tsp urad dal
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1 dried red chilli
  • few curry leaves
  • pinch hing / asafoetida
  • 2 chilli
  • 1 inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 potato
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Procedural steps

  1. Make a batter by adding required amount of water with the ingredients listed above
  2. firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil and splutter 1 tsp mustard, 1 tsp urad dal, 1 tsp chana dal, 1 dried red chilli, few curry leaves, pinch hing.
  3. now add 2 chilli and 1 inch ginger. saute well.
  4. also, add 1 onion and saute until onions shrink slightly.
  5. further, add 1/2 tsp turmeric and 1 tsp salt. saute well.
  6. now add 3 potato and mix well, mash slightly making sure everything is well combined.
  7. turn off the flame and add 2 tbsp coriander and 2 tbsp lemon juice.
  8. mix well and aloo bhaji for masala dosa is ready. keep aside.
  9. then in a pan,add a ladleful of batter.
  10. spread as thin as possible making a crispy dosa.
  11. take 1 tsp of butter and spread uniformly.
  12. also, place 2 tbsp of prepared aloo masala in the centre.
  13. roast until the dosa turns golden brown and crisp.
  14. scrape the sides of dosa and roll the dosa.
  15. finally, masala dosa recipe is ready to serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
